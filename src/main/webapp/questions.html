Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate?
You can donate by clicking the "Donate" button on the donation page and following the instructions. We accept payments via credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking.
2. Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, your donation is tax-deductible. You will receive a receipt for your donation, which you can use for tax purposes.
3. How will my donation be used?
Your donation will be used to support underprivileged communities by providing essential resources like food, education, and medical aid.
4. Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, you can choose to donate anonymously. Your name will not be displayed publicly.
5. How do I get a receipt for my donation?
A receipt will be automatically sent to the email address you provide during the donation process.
6. Can I cancel or modify my donation?
Unfortunately, donations cannot be canceled or modified once they are processed. Please double-check your details before confirming.
7. How can I track my donation?
You will receive regular updates via email about how your donation is being used. You can also log in to your account to track your contributions.
8. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets.
9. How can I contact support?
You can contact our support team by emailing support@example.com or calling +91-1234567890.
10. Can I set up recurring donations?
Yes, you can set up recurring donations during the payment process. Choose the "Recurring Donation" option and select the frequency.
